Carbonara Recipe

Granny's Favorite Carbonara Recipe

Luscious and wonderfully indulgent, pasta carbonara takes as long to make as it does to cook the pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil or unsalted butter
  • 1/2 pound pancetta or thick cut bacon, diced
  • 1-2 garlic cloves, minced, about 1 teaspoon(optional)
  • 3-4 whole eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan or pecorina cheese
  • 1 pound spaghetti (or bucatini or fettuccine)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Heat pasta water
  2. Saute pancetta/bacon and garlic
  3. Beat eggs and half of the cheese
  4. Cook pasta
  5. Toss pasta with pancetta/bacon
  6. Add the beaten egg mixture
